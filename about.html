About JMG

Pioneering Nigeria's Infrastructure Development Through Excellence in Engineering

Our Story

Founded in 2000, JMG has grown from a small engineering firm to become Nigeria's premier provider of comprehensive electro-mechanical solutions. We have consistently delivered world-class infrastructure projects that drive economic growth and improve quality of life across Nigeria.

Our commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction has established us as the trusted partner for major corporations, government agencies, and institutions throughout the country.
